Welcome to WORKMapper! The assets shown on the mapping application you are about to use represent utilty infrastructure. You can tell which assets have work orders associated with them through the purple outlines showing underneath an asset. These outlines will appear and disappear automatically as work orders are opened and closed.

To view more information about an asset, click or tap on one in the map. A sidebar will appear and the asset's attributes will be displayed in a menu.

From the sidebar, you can also create new work orders. Simply click the Create Work Order button at the bottom of the menu. Or you can click "close" to return to the map.

Click or tap off to the side of this dialog box to begin. Happy (work) mapping!
